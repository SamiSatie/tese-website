Structuring project

CTAF - Fractional-Supply Traction Chain

A low-voltage traction-chain concept designed for resilience and source hybridisation. The presentations mention 2 patents, 15 publications and 3 defended PhD theses around this programme.

Software

e-Pivot

Software developed jointly with Stellantis for vehicle-level optimisation of electric machines and traction chains.

  • Parametric e-motor model
  • Magnetic, electrical, mechanical and vehicle evaluation
  • 40 design parameters, 2 objectives, 10 constraints
