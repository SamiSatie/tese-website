Facilities

Characterisation and validation assets for electrical machines, conversion chains and traction systems.

Demonstrator

1 kW and 5 kW CTAF demonstrators

Fractional and sectorised synchronous-machine demonstrators controlled by several inverters.

  • 5 kW - 12 inverters
  • Dedicated load machines
Machine

Flux-switching machines

Permanent-magnet, wound-field and double-excitation topologies for embedded applications.

  • 2 kW class
  • Embedded applications
  • Fault-tolerant potential
Bench

High-speed bench

5 kW - 27,000 rpm bench with high-precision instrumentation for performance and loss measurements.

  • High precision
  • Driving-cycle operation
  • High-speed topologies
