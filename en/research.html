Research

TeSE activities address design, modelling, control and optimisation of electromechanical conversion chains.

Axis 1

Machines and transducers

Unconventional machine concepts, flux-switching machines, hybrid excitation and traction chains.

Axis 2

Modelling and optimisation

Finite-element models, analytical models, losses, uncertainties, parametric and topology optimisation.

Axis 3

Control and energy systems

Advanced control, diagnosis, digital twins, AC/DC grids and multi-vector systems.
