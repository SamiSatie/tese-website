Structuring project

CTAF - Fractional-Supply Traction Chain

A low-voltage fractional drive-chain concept designed for resilience, source hybridisation, reduced phase voltage and tighter machine-converter integration.

  • 2 patents filed
  • 15 conference and journal publications
  • 3 defended PhD theses
  • 8 workshops with industrial participation
Software

e-Pivot

A joint SATIE / Stellantis software tool developed since 2017 for vehicle-level optimisation of electrical machines and traction chains.

  • Parametric e-motor model
  • Magnetic, electrical, mechanical and vehicle-level evaluation
  • 40 design parameters, 2 objectives, 10 constraints
