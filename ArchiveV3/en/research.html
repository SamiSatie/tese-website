Research

TeSE activities are organised around design, control and optimisation of electrical systems and microsystems.

Theme

Concepts and conversion chains

New transducer concepts, resilient conversion chains, electric traction and source hybridisation.

  • Unconventional topologies
  • CTAF
  • Mutualisation and fractioning
Theme

Modelling and optimisation

Analytical, semi-analytical and topology-based approaches to optimal design of machines and conversion chains.

  • Eco-design
  • Multi-physics
  • Operating cycle
Theme

Control and digital twins

Real-time simulation, advanced control, diagnostics and digital twins for energy systems.

  • Digital implementation
  • FPGA
  • Resilient control
Shared backbone
